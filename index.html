Types of punches in Boxing

  • The Jab
  • Straight
  • Hook
  • Uppercut

There are multiple types of Punches in Boxing. Each type of punch have their own advantage and disadvantage. Study these techniques carefully so that you'll be able to devastate your opponent both physically and mentally in the boxing ring. By that, ill go over the types of punches in boxing in the columns below.

The Jab

The jab is one of the most basic yet important punch in boxing.They help you find the distant and range for the punch. Also, the jab helps you disrupt your opponents rythm so that you can go in and land for a clean shot. The main advantage of the Jab is that if used correctly you'll be able to control and dictate the fight. One disadvantage of the Jab though is that its not a powerful punch to throw if your looking for a knockout punch.

Well-known fighters who utilize the Jab

  • Muhammad Ali
  • Floyd Mayweather
  • Gennady Golovkin
  • George Foreman

The Cross (Straight)

The cross or what is called a "straight" is a power punch that is used to set up for a knockout. This punch is usually thrown with your dominant hand. One of the advantage of the Straight punch is that it is very lethal if the punch is timed correctly. A disadvantage of the punch is that if its not punched correctly it will be easy for an opponent to set up a nasty counter punch.

Well-known fighters who utilize the Cross

  • Manny Pacquiao
  • Thomas Hearns
  • Wladimir Klitschko
  • Joe Louis

The Hook

The hook is one of the most powerful punch in boxing. Boxers who perfect this punch usually have a high knockout rate. The main advantage of the hook is that you can use this punch anytime you want to settle for that big knockout punch. Now about the disadvantages, it is very difficult to land a clean shot with this punch.

Well-Known fighters who utilize the Hook

  • Sugar Ray Robinson
  • Oscar De La Hoya
  • Miguel Cotto
  • Joe Frazier

The Uppercut

Boxers who utilize this punch frequently have a reach disadvantage.By that, the uppercut makes it a useful punch when it comes to striking from close distance. Further adding, Uppercuts are very deceptive; for example, your opponent can be thrown off by thinking that you'll throw a hook but instead you'll come in with that filthy uppercut to the body/head and *BOOM* their down! Advantages to the punch is that you can use uppercuts to put down their guard so that you can throw a clean punch. Disadvantages is that you should never use this punch if your out of reach.

Well-Known fighters who utilize the Uppercut

  • Sugar Ray Leonard
  • Canelo Alvarez
  • Rocky Marciano
  • Mike Tyson
